About

In 2017, I discovered the term "nonbinary" and everything clicked: I am a nonbinary transmasc visual artist and graphic designer. My pronouns are he/they. In 2023, I return to Belgium, my native country, after eight years in London. I have tried several things, art remains the constant. I hope to enter a painting school at the end of 2024; I love dates and semicolons. See my artistic approach here.

Barbaroo, my feline daughter, needs kibble and clean litter. So please, hire me for:

  1. design your simple but personal websites;
  2. design your posters, menus, CVs, album covers;
  3. help with your garden: you can grow carrots, I believe in you;
  4. paint your portrait in a very me style or even just your living room;

Or maybe do you want to collaborate?

Glazier

Glazier is a monthly show on RTM.FM where I play whatever I want to listen to. It also means inviting friends to play what they want to listen to. It all began in January 2023. Each time (almost each time) I made a visual for the show for social media and the RTM website.
